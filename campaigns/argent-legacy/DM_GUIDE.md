# Argent Legacy — DM Guide

_Campaign-specific narrative rulebook. The engine's `session start` reads canonical
state from `state.json`; this guide holds the tone, world facts, and NPC roster the
engine doesn't model. Update it between sessions as the world changes._

---

## Tone & Genre

Heroic high-fantasy with a destiny arc: a young Warlock uncovering a celestial
lineage (the Argent Legacy) in the wake of a purified Blight. Wonder and growing
power, shadowed by the unknown cost of the inheritance. [DM: confirm — `meta.tone` is unset]

## Pacing Rules

- Combat: crisp and mechanical. Roll → engine → narrate. No extended monologues.
- Exploration: 1-2 sentences of atmosphere, then prompt for a player decision.
- Social scenes: let dialogue breathe; <=3 NPC lines before asking what the player does.
- Never resolve an ambiguous intent without asking ("I go to the market" — for what?).

## House Rules

Active in `state.json.houseRules`: `noFudging`, `diceReveal`, `enemyHpVisible`,
`pcRollsOwn` (the player rolls their PC's actions), `aiRollsWorld` (the DM rolls
NPCs/world). Otherwise SRD 5.1 as written.
