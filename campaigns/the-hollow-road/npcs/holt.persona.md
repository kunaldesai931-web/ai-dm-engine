## Notes

Holt is in his forties, works loading grain at the Crossing. He has a wife and two children. He is not a coward — he is a man who made a calculation and has to live with it.

He will not volunteer anything. If Aldric shows him the token, he will recognize it as a Vellmark company seal. He will say nothing useful unless he believes his silence is the greater danger — either because Aldric knows enough that Holt's cover is already blown, or because the threshold has moved against him again.

If he talks, he gives everything: the dock, the wagon, the number of people, the direction they traveled. It's specific and damning.

**DM hooks:**
- Perception DC 12 to notice he's been recently watching the street before he answers his door.
- If Sera sends the player to him, he'll slam the door without a word. Intimidation DC 16 or a demonstration that the player already knows enough to implicate him is the path through.
- If he talks, he asks for nothing — he's past the point of negotiating. He just wants it to matter.
